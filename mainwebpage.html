Welcomes You to

Deane Rd Tropical Garden Centre is situated in the heart of Bolton in Lancashire.

It offers a relaxing family day out. You can explore our Tropical Garden where you will be surrounded by a variety of plants and tree species. Our plants are carefully nurtured and grown with consideration to their natural environment.

Our Tropical shop sells a selection of seeds,roots,plants and products. These can be purchased at the centre or here online. You too could have a wonderful tropical plant in your own garden. We offer advice on all plants sold.

Enjoy a meal or snack in our restaurant. Childrens meals are available.

Children can take part in craft activities and learn about the tropical rainforests. All activities are supervised by our staff, all ages welcome and even adults can join in.
